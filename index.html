Help drive change and empower more people to talk about their mental health.

For just £2 a week, you can support one person to join a mental health swim. Support our community by donating or buying our merchandise.

Mental Health Swims is an award-winning, mental health peer support community.We create welcoming and inclusive swim groups for people struggling with their mental health. We are proud to have groups nationwide. 

We try to make it as easy as possible for you to join in whether you are a seasoned outdoor swimmer, a paddler or a toe dipper. Our friendly volunteers will welcome you on the day. 

Who are we?

We believe in empowering everyone to enjoy the healing power of cold water and community. 

Join us for a chat on the beach, a litter pick, a paddle or a swim in the shallows. We are an easy-going group that prefers short dips to long distances and puts companionship before competitive swimming. We aim to provide an inclusive space to people of all skin colours, body shapes, sexualities, genders, backgrounds, ages and abilities.
